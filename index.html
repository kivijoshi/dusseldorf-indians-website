Welcome to Düsseldorf Indians

Your trusted source for expat information, community, and services in Düsseldorf. Explore essential resources, connect with the community, and find everything you need for a smooth expat experience in Düsseldorf.

Important Government Offices

Bürgerbüro (Citizens' Office): Official Website For Anmeldung (address registration), ID, passport, and other citizen services.
Ausländerbehörde (Foreigners' Office): Official Website Residence permits, visa, and expat immigration services.
Finanzamt Düsseldorf (Tax Office): Official Website Tax ID, tax returns, and all tax-related matters for residents.
Standesamt (Registry Office): Official Website Birth, marriage, and death certificates.
Jobcenter Düsseldorf: Official Website Unemployment benefits, job search, and integration support.
Serviceportal Düsseldorf: Digital Service Portal Online appointments, digital forms, and city services for expats.

First Steps in Düsseldorf

  1. Register your address (Anmeldung) at the Bürgerbüro.
  2. Open a German bank account.
  3. Get health insurance (public or private).
  4. Apply for a residence permit (if required).
  5. Register for tax ID at the Finanzamt.
  6. Explore local transport and get a monthly ticket.
